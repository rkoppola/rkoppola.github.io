Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the "Balloon Popper AR" app (the "Service") created by Raj Koppola. This service is intended for use by children and families.

Commitment to Children's Privacy

We are committed to protecting the privacy of children who use our app. This policy explains our information practices in accordance with the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

Data Collection

Personal Information

We do not knowingly collect, store, or share any personal information from children. We do not require users to sign in, create an account, or provide names, addresses, emails, or phone numbers.

Camera Usage (Augmented Reality)

The app requires access to your device's camera to enable Augmented Reality (AR) features. This data is processed locally on your device by Apple's ARKit. We do not record, save, or transmit any video or images from your camera. The camera feed is used solely to display virtual balloons in your real-world environment.

AdMob (Advertising)

We use Google AdMob to display age-appropriate advertisements to support the free version of the app. We have configured AdMob to comply with COPPA regulations by enabling the "Tag for Child Directed Treatment" setting.

This means:

  • Google will not serve personalized ads (behavioral advertising) to users of this app.
  • Ads shown will be based on context (the app content) rather than user data.
  • No user identifiers (like IDFA) are used to track users across other apps or websites.

Information Sharing

Since we do not collect personal information, we do not share any personal information with third parties.

Parental Control

If you believe we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately so we can remove strict measures to delete it.
