Collecting and Using Your Personal Data

Camera Usage

Our Application requires access to your device's camera to function. The camera is used solely for the purpose of providing Augmented Reality (AR) features to help you mark cricket field boundaries. We do not record, store, or transmit any video footage or images from your camera to our servers or any third party. All processing happens locally on your device.
